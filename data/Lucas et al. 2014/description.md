Data digitized from Figure 1(b) of paper:

Lucas, J., Schouman, A., Lyphout, L., Cousin, X. and Lefrancois, C. (2014), Allometric relationship between body mass and aerobic metabolism in zebrafish Danio rerio. J Fish Biol, 84: 1171-1178. [https://doi.org/10.1111/jfb.12306](https://doi.org/10.1111/jfb.12306)



The figure shows multiple datapoints of body mass (in g) vs active metabolic rate (in mgO2/h) for larvae (5 dpf), juveniles (60 dpf), adults (180 dpf). 



The temperature was 28ºC.



The authors also report length and weight, with standard errors, at the same ages.
